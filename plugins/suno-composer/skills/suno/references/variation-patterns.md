# Song Variation Patterns

## Core Principle
**Preserve:** Hook, core melody, lyrical theme, emotional intent
**Transform:** Arrangement, production, tempo, energy level

## Variation Types

### Acoustic Version
**Intent:** Organic, intimate, stripped-back transformation

**Style Prompt Modifications:**
- Remove: electronic, synth, compressed, polished, EDM elements
- Add: acoustic, organic, intimate, live feel, warm, natural reverb
- Tempo: Often 10-20% slower

**Arrangement Changes:**
- Lead: Acoustic guitar or piano replaces synths
- Bass: Upright bass or acoustic bass
- Drums: Brushes, minimal percussion, or none
- Add: String accents (optional)

**Metatag Adaptations:**
```
[Verse][Acoustic guitar only]
[Chorus][Add light percussion]
[Bridge][stripped][Voice and piano]
```

**Production Tags:** lo-fi, warm, natural reverb, intimate, organic, live feel

---

### Remix Version
**Intent:** Electronic/dance transformation, club-ready

**Style Prompt Modifications:**
- Remove: acoustic, organic, intimate, ballad elements
- Add: electronic, dance, remix, four-on-the-floor, energetic, club
- Tempo: Often 120-130 BPM (house) or 140 BPM (dubstep feel)

**Arrangement Changes:**
- Lead: Synth replaces acoustic instruments
- Bass: 808 or synthesized bass
- Drums: Electronic kit, side-chain compression
- Add: Risers, drops, builds

**Metatag Adaptations:**
```
[Intro: Build, filtered]
[Verse][four-on-the-floor]
[Drop][Full electronic arrangement]
[Break][stripped][snare roll]
```

**Production Tags:** compressed, side-chained, wide stereo, punchy, crisp, electronic

---

### Stripped Version
**Intent:** Minimal arrangement, vocal showcase

**Style Prompt Modifications:**
- Remove: full arrangement, layered, orchestral, complex
- Add: minimal, stripped, vocal-focused, intimate, exposed
- Tempo: Original or slightly slower

**Arrangement Changes:**
- Lead: Voice prominently exposed
- Accompaniment: Single instrument (piano OR guitar)
- Remove: All other instruments, harmonies (or minimal)

**Metatag Adaptations:**
```
[Verse][Voice and piano only]
[Chorus][Minimal accompaniment]
[Bridge][a cappella]
```

**Production Tags:** dry vocal, intimate, minimal, exposed, close-mic, quiet

---

### Extended Version
**Intent:** Full arrangement with added sections

**Style Prompt Modifications:**
- Keep: Original style as base
- Add: extended, full arrangement, epic, building, dynamic
- Tempo: Original

**Arrangement Changes:**
- Add: Extended intro (instrumental, 16-32 bars)
- Add: Instrumental break/solo section
- Add: Extended bridge with key change
- Add: Longer outro with callbacks
- All original instruments plus layers

**Metatag Adaptations:**
```
[Extended Intro: Instrumental build, 32 bars]
[Verse 1]
[Instrumental Break][Guitar solo]
[Bridge][key change up]
[Final Chorus][Full band, choir]
[Extended Outro: Fade]
```

**Production Tags:** full arrangement, layered, dynamic, building, epic, polished

---

### Cinematic Version
**Intent:** Orchestral, epic, dramatic transformation

**Style Prompt Modifications:**
- Remove: lo-fi, minimal, stripped, intimate
- Add: cinematic, orchestral, epic, dramatic, film score, sweeping
- Tempo: Often slower for drama, or maintained for action

**Arrangement Changes:**
- Add: Full strings section (violins, violas, cellos)
- Add: Brass accents (optional)
- Add: Timpani, orchestral percussion
- Keep: Original melodic elements as themes
- Dynamics: Extreme contrast (pp to ff)

**Metatag Adaptations:**
```
[Intro: Orchestral swell, strings]
[Verse][strings only]
[Chorus][full orchestra]
[Bridge][half-time][Brass enters]
[Final Chorus][key change up][Choir joins]
[Outro][Strings fade]
```

**Production Tags:** orchestral, cinematic, epic, wide stereo, dynamic, dramatic, film score

## Transformation Matrix

| Element | Acoustic | Remix | Stripped | Extended | Cinematic |
|---------|----------|-------|----------|----------|-----------|
| Tempo | -10-20% | +10-30% | Same/-5% | Same | Same/-10% |
| Energy | Lower | Higher | Lowest | Highest | Varies |
| Instruments | Organic | Electronic | 1-2 only | All + more | Orchestral |
| Production | Warm, lo-fi | Compressed | Dry, close | Full, layered | Wide, dynamic |
| Complexity | Simpler | Different | Minimal | Maximal | Elaborate |
