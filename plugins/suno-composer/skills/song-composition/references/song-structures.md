# Song Structure Patterns

Templates for common song structures optimized for Suno AI.

## Standard Pop Structure

The most common structure for pop, rock, and J-pop songs:

```
[Intro] - 4-8 bars
[Verse 1] - 8-16 bars
[Pre-chorus] - 4-8 bars
[Chorus] - 8-16 bars
[Verse 2] - 8-16 bars
[Pre-chorus] - 4-8 bars
[Chorus] - 8-16 bars
[Bridge] - 8 bars
[Final Chorus] - 8-16 bars
[Outro] - 4-8 bars
```

**Use for:** Most pop songs, J-pop, rock, K-pop

## Japanese Pop Variations

### Anime Opening Style (90 Seconds)

Designed for TV-size anime openings:

```
[Intro] - Short instrumental hook (4 bars)
[Verse 1] - Quick setup (8 bars)
[Chorus] - Catchy, memorable (8-12 bars)
[Verse 2] - Development (8 bars)
[Chorus] - With variation (8-12 bars)
[Outro] - Instrumental fade or tag (4 bars)
```

**Characteristics:**
- Front-loaded energy (hook in first 15 seconds)
- Catchy chorus that hits early
- Fast transitions between sections
- ~90 second total runtime

### Ballad Structure

Builds slowly to emotional climax:

```
[Intro] - Atmospheric, sets mood (4-8 bars)
[Verse 1] - Soft, intimate (8-16 bars)
[Verse 2] - Building slightly (8-16 bars)
[Chorus] - Emotional release (8-16 bars)
[Verse 3] - Deeper lyrics (8-16 bars)
[Chorus] - More intensity (8-16 bars)
[Bridge] - Key change or breakdown (8 bars)
[Final Chorus] - Full emotional climax (8-16 bars)
[Outro] - Gentle resolution (4-8 bars)
```

**Characteristics:**
- Longer verses for storytelling
- Multiple verses before first chorus
- Delayed climax for emotional impact
- Key change in bridge or final chorus

## EDM/Dance Structure

Build-drop pattern for electronic music:

```
[Intro] - Atmospheric/filtered (8-16 bars)
[Build 1] - Rising tension (8 bars)
[Drop 1] - Main hook (16 bars)
[Breakdown] - Stripped, melodic (8-16 bars)
[Build 2] - Bigger tension (8 bars)
[Drop 2] - Variation of main hook (16 bars)
[Outro] - Fade out (8 bars)
```

**Characteristics:**
- Clear build-drop pattern
- Drops carry the main energy
- Breakdowns provide contrast
- Less traditional verse/chorus

## Rock/Metal Structure

Power-driven with solo section:

```
[Intro] - Riff or atmospheric (4-8 bars)
[Verse 1] - Lower energy (8-16 bars)
[Pre-chorus] - Building (4-8 bars)
[Chorus] - Full power (8-16 bars)
[Verse 2] - Return to lower energy (8-16 bars)
[Pre-chorus] - (4-8 bars)
[Chorus] - (8-16 bars)
[Bridge/Solo] - Guitar solo or breakdown (8-16 bars)
[Final Chorus] - Often extended (12-24 bars)
[Outro] - Fade or hard ending (4-8 bars)
```

**Characteristics:**
- Strong riff-based intro
- Dynamic contrast between verse and chorus
- Solo section common
- Endings can be abrupt or fade

## K-pop Structure

Blends pop with rap/dance breaks:

```
[Intro] - Hook or dance break (4-8 bars)
[Verse 1] - Often includes rap (8-16 bars)
[Pre-chorus] - Building (4-8 bars)
[Chorus] - Hook-driven (8-16 bars)
[Verse 2] - Different member/style (8-16 bars)
[Pre-chorus] - (4-8 bars)
[Chorus] - (8-16 bars)
[Bridge] - Often stripped or key change (8 bars)
[Dance Break] - Instrumental (4-8 bars)
[Final Chorus] - (8-16 bars)
[Outro] - Hook callback (4 bars)
```

**Characteristics:**
- Multiple sections per "verse" (rap + sung)
- Dance breaks for performance
- Key changes common
- Strong hook repetition

## Section Duration Guidelines

| Tempo | Short Section | Medium Section | Long Section |
|-------|---------------|----------------|--------------|
| Slow (60-80 BPM) | 4-8 bars | 8-12 bars | 12-16 bars |
| Mid (80-120 BPM) | 4-8 bars | 8-16 bars | 16-24 bars |
| Fast (120-180 BPM) | 4 bars | 8 bars | 12-16 bars |

**General rule:** Faster tempos = shorter sections feel appropriate
