# Genre Deep Dive

## J-pop Subgenres

### Shibuya-kei
90s-influenced sophisticated pop with jazz and bossa nova elements.
**Tags:** shibuya-kei, jazzy, sophisticated, retro, french pop influence, bossa nova

### City Pop
80s Japanese pop with funk, boogie, and AOR influences.
**Tags:** city pop, 80s, funky bass, saxophone, disco, japanese boogie, nostalgic, groovy

### Visual Kei
Rock with dramatic, theatrical presentation.
**Tags:** visual kei, j-rock, dramatic, gothic, theatrical, powerful

### Anime/Game Soundtrack
Music designed for visual media, often genre-blending.
**Tags:** anime soundtrack, cinematic, orchestral, epic, emotional, game music

### Idol Pop
Bright, youthful pop for idol groups.
**Tags:** idol pop, cute, bright, cheerful, group vocals, danceable, energetic

## Electronic Subgenres

### Future Bass
Melodic electronic with heavy synths and emotional progressions.
**Tags:** future bass, electronic, synth heavy, emotional, melodic drop, atmospheric

### Synthwave/Retrowave
80s-inspired electronic with nostalgic synth sounds.
**Tags:** synthwave, retrowave, 80s synth, nostalgic, neon, driving

### Kawaii Future Bass
Cute, bright electronic with anime influences.
**Tags:** kawaii future bass, cute electronic, bright synths, anime, happy

### Hardcore/Denpa
Fast, intense electronic often with high-pitched vocals.
**Tags:** denpa, hardcore, fast electronic, intense, high energy, otaku

## Rock Subgenres

### J-rock
Japanese rock with melodic sensibilities.
**Tags:** j-rock, rock, guitar driven, melodic, powerful, japanese rock

### Alternative/Indie
Less mainstream rock with experimental elements.
**Tags:** alternative, indie rock, artistic, experimental, lo-fi

### Post-rock
Instrumental rock with atmospheric builds.
**Tags:** post-rock, instrumental, atmospheric, building, cinematic, ambient rock

### Metal/Heavy
Heavy guitar-driven music.
**Tags:** j-metal, heavy, distorted guitars, intense, aggressive, powerful

## Ballad Variations

### Piano Ballad
Intimate, piano-focused emotional songs.
**Tags:** piano ballad, intimate, emotional, soft, heartfelt, minimal

### Orchestral Ballad
Grand, sweeping emotional pieces.
**Tags:** orchestral ballad, strings, cinematic, epic emotional, dramatic

### Acoustic Ballad
Guitar-driven gentle songs.
**Tags:** acoustic ballad, acoustic guitar, gentle, warm, intimate, folk

### Power Ballad
Building from soft to powerful.
**Tags:** power ballad, building, emotional climax, powerful vocals, rock ballad

## Folk/Traditional Fusion

### J-folk
Japanese folk with modern sensibilities.
**Tags:** j-folk, folk, acoustic, storytelling, warm, traditional elements

### Enka-influenced
Traditional Japanese vocal style elements.
**Tags:** enka influence, traditional japanese, emotional vibrato, melancholic, nostalgic

### World Fusion
Blending Japanese and international folk elements.
**Tags:** world music, fusion, ethnic instruments, global, eclectic

## R&B/Soul

### J-R&B
Japanese R&B with smooth production.
**Tags:** j-rnb, smooth, soulful, groovy, urban, r&b

### Neo-Soul
Modern soul with jazz influences.
**Tags:** neo soul, jazzy, soulful, organic, warm, sophisticated

## Hip-Hop/Rap

### J-Hip-Hop
Japanese hip-hop styles.
**Tags:** j-hiphop, rap, urban, beats, flow, japanese hip hop

### Lo-fi Hip-Hop
Chill, sample-based beats.
**Tags:** lo-fi hip hop, chill, jazzy samples, relaxed, study music

## Experimental/Avant-garde

### Art Pop
Experimental pop with artistic vision.
**Tags:** art pop, experimental, avant-garde, artistic, unconventional

### Noise Pop
Melodic songs with noise elements.
**Tags:** noise pop, shoegaze, distorted, dreamy, wall of sound

### Ambient Pop
Atmospheric, texture-focused pop.
**Tags:** ambient pop, atmospheric, ethereal, spacious, dreamy, textured
