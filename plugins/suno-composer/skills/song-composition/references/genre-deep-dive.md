# Genre Deep Dive

## J-pop Subgenres

### Shibuya-kei
90s-influenced sophisticated pop with jazz and bossa nova elements.
**Tags:** shibuya-kei, jazzy, sophisticated, retro, french pop influence, bossa nova

### City Pop
80s Japanese pop with funk, boogie, and AOR influences.
**Tags:** city pop, 80s, funky bass, saxophone, disco, japanese boogie, nostalgic, groovy

### Visual Kei
Rock with dramatic, theatrical presentation.
**Tags:** visual kei, j-rock, dramatic, gothic, theatrical, powerful

### Anime/Game Soundtrack
Music designed for visual media, often genre-blending.
**Tags:** anime soundtrack, cinematic, orchestral, epic, emotional, game music

### Idol Pop
Bright, youthful pop for idol groups.
**Tags:** idol pop, cute, bright, cheerful, group vocals, danceable, energetic

### Kawaii Future Bass

## Western Pop

### Mainstream Pop
Radio-friendly pop with polished production (Taylor Swift, Dua Lipa style).
**Tags:** pop, mainstream, catchy, polished production, radio friendly, upbeat
**Tempo:** 100-128 BPM
**Production:** compressed, wide stereo, crisp drums, layered vocals

### Indie Pop
Alternative-leaning pop with artistic sensibilities (Lorde, Billie Eilish style).
**Tags:** indie pop, alternative, artistic, minimalist, dark pop, atmospheric
**Tempo:** 80-120 BPM
**Production:** lo-fi elements, sparse, intimate, reverb-heavy

### Synth-Pop
Electronic-driven pop with prominent synthesizers.
**Tags:** synth pop, electronic, 80s influence, synthesizer, dance pop, retro
**Tempo:** 110-130 BPM
**Production:** analog synths, gated reverb, punchy drums

### Acoustic Pop
Stripped-down pop centered on acoustic instruments.
**Tags:** acoustic pop, singer-songwriter, warm, intimate, guitar-driven, organic
**Tempo:** 80-110 BPM
**Production:** natural reverb, minimal processing, live feel

### Dance Pop
High-energy pop designed for dancing.
**Tags:** dance pop, club, energetic, four-on-the-floor, infectious, party
**Tempo:** 120-135 BPM
**Production:** side-chained bass, punchy kick, bright synths

### Indie Rock

### Soft Rock / Adult Contemporary
Mellow rock suitable for easy listening.

### Power Pop

**Tempo:** 120-150 BPM
**Production:** crisp, jangly guitars, tight drums, layered harmonies

### Post-Punk Revival

## EDM / Electronic Dance Music

### House
Four-on-the-floor dance music with soulful elements.
**Tags:** house, four-on-the-floor, groovy, dancefloor, soulful, disco influence
**Tempo:** 120-130 BPM
**Production:** pumping bass, warm pads, funky percussion

### Deep House
Atmospheric, laid-back house with jazzy elements.
**Tags:** deep house, atmospheric, jazzy, smooth, late night, chill dance
**Tempo:** 118-125 BPM
**Production:** warm bass, subtle pads, organic percussion, reverb

### Progressive House
Building, melodic house with emotional progressions.
**Tags:** progressive house, melodic, building, emotional, euphoric, festival
**Tempo:** 125-130 BPM
**Production:** layered synths, long builds, side-chain compression, wide stereo

### Techno
Minimalist, hypnotic electronic with industrial edge.
**Tags:** techno, minimal, industrial, hypnotic, dark, underground
**Tempo:** 125-140 BPM
**Production:** 808/909 drums, acid bass, minimal elements, driving

### Dubstep
Heavy bass music with half-time rhythms and wobble bass.
**Tags:** dubstep, bass heavy, wobble, aggressive, half-time, intense
**Tempo:** 140 BPM (half-time feel at 70)
**Production:** massive sub bass, LFO modulation, compressed, punchy

### Drum & Bass
Fast breakbeat-driven electronic with heavy bass.
**Tags:** drum and bass, dnb, breakbeats, fast, bass heavy, jungle
**Tempo:** 160-180 BPM
**Production:** rolling drums, reese bass, atmospheric pads

### Trance
Euphoric, melodic electronic with driving arpeggios.
**Tags:** trance, euphoric, melodic, arpeggios, uplifting, epic buildup
**Tempo:** 130-145 BPM
**Production:** supersaw leads, layered pads, gated reverb, wide mix

### Big Room / Festival EDM
High-energy festival music with massive drops.
**Tags:** big room, festival, edm, massive drop, high energy, anthem
**Tempo:** 125-130 BPM
**Production:** huge kicks, white noise risers, compressed, mono-compatible

### Future House
Bouncy, metallic house with pitched vocal chops.
**Tags:** future house, bouncy, metallic bass, vocal chops, modern, groovy
**Tempo:** 125-128 BPM
**Production:** pitched bass, chopped vocals, crisp hi-hats

### Hardstyle
Intense, hard-hitting electronic with distorted kicks.
**Tags:** hardstyle, hard dance, distorted kick, intense, euphoric, hard
**Tempo:** 150-160 BPM
**Production:** reverse bass, distorted kick, screeches, compressed

## K-pop

### Boy Group Style
High-energy K-pop with rap sections and synchronized choreography feel (BTS, Stray Kids style).
**Tags:** k-pop, boy group, energetic, hip-hop influence, powerful, synchronized
**Tempo:** 100-140 BPM
**Production:** polished, layered, trap elements, crisp, maximalist

### Girl Group Style
Catchy, polished K-pop with strong hooks (BLACKPINK, TWICE style).
**Tags:** k-pop, girl group, catchy, powerful, glamorous, hook-driven
**Tempo:** 100-130 BPM
**Production:** bright, punchy, bass-heavy, polished vocals

### K-R&B
Smooth Korean R&B with Western influence.
**Tags:** k-rnb, korean rnb, smooth, soulful, urban, sensual
**Tempo:** 70-100 BPM
**Production:** warm, minimal beats, soft synths, intimate vocals

### K-Ballad
Emotional Korean ballads with powerful vocals.
**Tags:** k-ballad, korean ballad, emotional, powerful vocals, orchestral, heartfelt
**Tempo:** 60-80 BPM
**Production:** piano-driven, strings, dynamic, building climax

### K-Hip-Hop
Korean hip-hop with trap and boom-bap influences.
**Tags:** k-hiphop, korean rap, trap, hard hitting, urban, flex
**Tempo:** 80-160 BPM (varies)
**Production:** 808 bass, hi-hat rolls, minimalist, punchy

### Common K-pop Romanized Phrases
Use these for authentic K-pop flavor in English-dominant lyrics:

**Hooks/Exclamations:**
- saranghae (사랑해) - I love you
- gaja (가자) - let's go
- mwo (뭐) - what
- wae (왜) - why
- ani (아니) - no
- ne (네) - yes
- omo (오모) - oh my
- daebak (대박) - awesome
- fighting/hwaiting (화이팅) - you can do it

**Emotional Vocabulary:**
- nunmul (눈물) - tears
- maeum (마음) - heart/mind
- kkum (꿈) - dream
- bam (밤) - night
- byeol (별) - star
- neomu (너무) - so/very
- uri (우리) - us/we
- neo (너) - you

## Latin

### Reggaeton
Urban Latin music with dembow rhythm.
**Tags:** reggaeton, dembow, urban latin, perreo, latin trap, party
**Tempo:** 85-100 BPM
**Production:** dembow beat, 808 bass, punchy, spanish vocals

### Latin Pop
Mainstream Latin pop with crossover appeal.
**Tags:** latin pop, spanish pop, romantic, catchy, crossover, radio latin
**Tempo:** 90-120 BPM
**Production:** polished, acoustic guitar accents, warm, balanced

### Bachata
Romantic Dominican style with signature guitar.
**Tags:** bachata, romantic latin, dominican, sensual, guitar-driven, bongos
**Tempo:** 120-140 BPM
**Production:** nylon guitar, bongos, bass, romantic, intimate

### Salsa
Energetic Cuban-influenced dance music.
**Tags:** salsa, cuban, tropical, brass section, percussion heavy, danceable
**Tempo:** 160-220 BPM
**Production:** brass, congas, timbales, piano montuno, live feel

### Cumbia
Folkloric rhythm with modern adaptations.
**Tags:** cumbia, folk latin, accordion, guacharaca, tropical, festive
**Tempo:** 80-110 BPM
**Production:** accordion, scraper percussion, bass, organic

### Latin Trap
Trap music with Latin influences and Spanish lyrics.
**Tags:** latin trap, trap latino, 808, hard, urban, spanish rap
**Tempo:** 60-80 BPM (half-time)
**Production:** heavy 808, hi-hat rolls, dark, minimal

### Bossa Nova
Brazilian jazz-influenced style with gentle rhythm.
**Tags:** bossa nova, brazilian, jazzy, gentle, sophisticated, warm
**Tempo:** 120-140 BPM
**Production:** nylon guitar, soft drums, upright bass, warm, intimate

### Common Spanish Phrases for Latin Songs
Use these for authentic Latin flavor:

**Romantic:**
- mi amor - my love
- corazón - heart
- te quiero - I want you / I love you
- mi vida - my life
- bésame - kiss me
- contigo - with you

**Party/Energy:**
- dale - go/let's go
- fuego - fire
- loco/loca - crazy
- bailar - to dance
- fiesta - party
- caliente - hot

**Emotional:**
- dolor - pain
- lágrimas - tears
- sueño - dream
- cielo - sky/heaven
- noche - night
- solo/sola - alone
