emotional j-pop ballad, anime ending theme influence, slow tempo around 72 bpm, soft female vocals building to powerful emotional delivery, piano-driven with orchestral strings joining in chorus, reverb-heavy atmospheric mix, bittersweet melancholic mood with hopeful resolution, polished production with dynamic range

## Specifications

- **Tempo:** 72 BPM, slow ballad feel
- **Vocal:** Female, soft intimate verses building to powerful emotional chorus
- **Dynamics:** Soft verse (piano only) → Building pre-chorus → Full chorus → Stripped bridge → Climactic final chorus
- **Key Instruments:** Piano (lead), orchestral strings, subtle percussion in chorus
- **Production Style:** Reverb-heavy, polished, wide stereo field, dynamic range preserved

---

**Note:** This example demonstrates selective metatag usage:
- Most sections have only the section marker
- `[Bridge][stripped, piano only]` - arrangement change
- `[Final Chorus][full arrangement, strings swell]` - arrangement change
- No cumulative intensity tags that cause pitch drift
